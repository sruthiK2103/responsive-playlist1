Best Professors Around!

Our University has the best professors teaching courses with hands on practical sessions.The heartfelt commitment of our faculty and staff to your development, both in and beyond the classroom, is one of Northwest's finest assets.

Help Sessions

Specific hours in a week are alloted for each course to help students with their questions and doubts. Graduate Assistants for the course will be assisting the students to clarify their doubts.

Scholarships

Students are motivated by offering scholarships and fee waivers depending on the GPA they maintain each semester and also provided with on-campus jobs like Graduate and Research assistants.

Laptop/Textbook Program

Graduate students (at least 9 credit hours) will receive a fully loaded laptop to use while studying at our University. This way our students save time and money by not having to find, buy and resell textbooks, and on-campus technical support is provided to students.

Campus Dining

Our campus dining is the perfect all-you-care-to-eat restaurant. This dining location includes a Mediterranean kitchen, an all-American grill, Tex-Mex cuisine, an Entree station offering home-style meals, exhibition station, and Mongolian grill as well as a fresh bakery.

Wellness Services

Our wellness Services focuses on the six dimensions of wellness (physical, social, emotional, spiritual, intellectual, occupational) of every student, every day, through inspiration, promotion, and knowledge. All services conducted by Wellness Services are kept confidential.
